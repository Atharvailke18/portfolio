About Me

Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Atharva Satappa Ilke, a 21-year-old designer specializing in poster creation and digital graphics. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from Mumbai University and have been passionately pursuing design since 2023.

My Design Journey

What started as a hobby has grown into a professional passion. Over the past two years, I've honed my skills in graphic design, specializing in posters and digital media. My work blends technical knowledge from my IT background with creative design principles.

Professional Approach

I believe design should be both beautiful and functional. Every project I undertake begins with understanding the client's vision and ends with designs that communicate effectively while maintaining aesthetic appeal.
